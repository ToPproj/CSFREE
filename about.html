About CS Expert Roadmap

This program is designed to be a complete, self-directed Computer Science education built from free, high-quality resources. The roadmap organizes learning into progressive stages with recommended projects and mentoring options.

Mission

To democratize CS education by curating university-level materials and combining them with guidance, practice, and optional mentorship.

How to use this site

  • Start at Stage 1 and progress through the stages sequentially, or pick a track (Systems, ML, Theory).
  • Mark courses as complete to track progress and surface suggested projects.
  • Use the Support page to book tutoring or mentorship if you want guided learning.
