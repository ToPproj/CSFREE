Need help completing a course?

If you'd like guided support through any of these courses, we offer professional tutoring and coaching packages tailored to your goals — from focused 1:1 sessions to structured multi-week mentorship plans.

  • One-on-One Tutoring — Personalized sessions to target gaps, code reviews, and hands-on problem solving.
  • Structured Mentorship — Multi-week plans with milestones, project reviews, and career guidance.
  • Group Workshops — Cohort-based workshops for topic deep-dives and peer learning.
  • Interview Prep — Algorithm walkthroughs, mock interviews, and feedback reports.

How it works

  1. Book a free 15-minute consultation to discuss objectives and fit.
  2. Choose a plan: ad-hoc sessions, monthly mentoring, or an intensive bootcamp.
  3. Schedule sessions at convenient times; receive materials and progress tracking.
